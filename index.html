About

Building the future of
responsible AI

I'm a researcher and engineer passionate about making AI systems smarter, more honest, and more sustainable. My work spans LLM evaluation, sustainable computing, and personalized AI systems.

Currently at DFKI, I lead development on EAVE — a decision-support system for measuring and optimizing energy consumption and CO₂ emissions of AI workloads in data centers.

I hold a Master's in Computer Science from Universität des Saarlandes and a B.Tech from Cochin University of Science and Technology.

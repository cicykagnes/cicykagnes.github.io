Currently, I am a Research Assistant at the NLP Lab, German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) Berlin, and a Master's Thesis Student at the Data Science and Applications Lab, DFKI Kaiserslautern.

About Me

Making AI more responsible, efficient, and beautiful.

I am a computer science researcher and engineer passionate about building AI systems that are useful, reliable, and sustainable.

At DFKI, I work on sustainable AI operations, LLM workloads, and decision-support systems for measuring and optimizing energy consumption, CO₂ emissions, and operational cost in data centers.

My research also explores LLM fidelity, hallucination detection, knowledge graphs, federated learning, and personalized AI for healthcare and well-being.
